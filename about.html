      Biography

      Olebogeng Gideon Ramose(born april 28,1999,) better known by his stage name Youngskillsbeats. 

      Youngskillsbeats is a South African Record Producer, Performing Artist, Sound Designer, Content Creator, Entrepreneur, Musicologist and 
      Engineer From the Northwest of Mafikeng (Lomanyaneng) whose production credits includes 
      the Likes of Dee Xclsv ,NiccLarue ,Nipsey Blunt,Vato the Plug ,DanielRNuttall  , Lection
      ,Mabizol, T'mo Kangkane, Talgo, Veesmash, Bunleen and Many More. Youngskillsbeats Produces a Sound he Calls Tswakness.

      The sound was Inspired By the Music he Grew up listening to genres like:Motswako , Kwaito ,Classic ,Boombap ,Country,Blues, Jazz ,Reggae ,Heavy Metal  And Hiphop.                                                                               
      I Look up to Creatives Like HHP ,Cassper Nyovest ,JR ,Khuli Chana ,Tuks ,Dr Dre ,Curtiss King ,Eminem ,Snoop Dog ,Tkzee ,Lebo Mathosa ,ProKid ,Mandoza ,KB mamosadi ,Bob Marley ,Skwatta Kamp ,Kendrick lamer ,J Cole ,Erykah Badu, Bill Withers ,The Animals ,Linkin Park ,Mshoza ,Thebe ,Brenda Fassie ,Mzambiya ,Zola 7 ,Brickz ,Spikiri ,Mo'Lemi ,M'Du ,Brothers of Peace ,Malaika ,Trompies ,Cheese Kop ,Notshi ,Fifi Cooper ,Kaygizm ,Mapaputsi ,Sgonondo ,Brown Dash ,Ts All Stars ,Skeem ,Pitch Black Afro ,Dj Cleo ,Tokollo ,S'bu and many More .the Rest is History

      Had multiple Performances Starting out which were never Documented, But this was my First Documented Performance back in 2015.

      The Year was 2016 When I was Featured on Hope FM

      The Year was 2016 When I was on Tour Sponsored by Cell C

      Featured on Dj Mphoe's Music video , Fifi Coopers Music video which Appeared on Channel O and many more.

      The year was 2019 at a Recording Studio Based in Houghton, I Spent 2 years in that studio Learning New things that helped me improve myself as a Creator and Artist.

      Used to be someone who was closed minded and never wanted to try new things ,Such as Learning a Musical instrument . I Had a Piano from 2016 but never really knew how to play it, This was when I decided to Go to Collage and Study Sound Engineering. When I got there I felt so bad for not doing this long time ago, Being there made me Understand Music/Sound and its Theory. Learning Music Theory Raised my interested by me wanting more and Learning how to play every instrument, the year was 2019.

       Now a Sound Engineering Graduate, Who has Worked with Various  Industry Artists. Some are International Some are Local, The only way for you to Find out is to Stream my Music or check the Credits, year 2021.
